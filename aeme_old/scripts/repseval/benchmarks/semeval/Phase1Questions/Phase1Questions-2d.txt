Question 2(d) PART-WHOLE: Event:Feature

Consider the following word pairs:

rodeo:cowboy
banquet:food
wedding:bride

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

an X and Y are a similar type of action/thing/attribute
X prevents Y
X is intended to produce Y
Y is typically found at an event such as X

Correct Answer:

Y is typically found at an event such as X
