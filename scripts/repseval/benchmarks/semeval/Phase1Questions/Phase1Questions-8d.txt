Question 8(d) CAUSE-PURPOSE: Action/Activity:Goal

Consider the following word pairs:

eat:satiation flee:escape
fertilize:grow

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

someone/something will X in order to Y
a Y is used on an X
an X is a place/location/area where Y is made/done/produced
Y is an unacceptable form of X

Correct Answer:

someone/something will X in order to Y
