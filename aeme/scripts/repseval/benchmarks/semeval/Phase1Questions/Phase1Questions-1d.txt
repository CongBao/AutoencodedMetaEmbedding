Question 1(d) CLASS-INCLUSION: Plural Collective

Consider the following word pairs:

groceries:eggs
dishes:saucers
refreshments:sandwiches

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

an X cannot have attribute Y; Y is antithetical to being X
Y are items in a collection/group of X
Y is typically found at an event such as X
X will become / be converted into Y

Correct Answer:

Y are items in a collection/group of X
