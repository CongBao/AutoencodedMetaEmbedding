Question 7(c) CASE RELATIONS: Agent:Instrument

Consider the following word pairs:

farmer:tractor
conductor:baton
arsonist:match

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

an X is a place/location/area where Y is found
X is the name for knowledge of Y
an X uses Y to perform their role
Y is typically found at an event such as X

Correct Answer:

an X uses Y to perform their role
