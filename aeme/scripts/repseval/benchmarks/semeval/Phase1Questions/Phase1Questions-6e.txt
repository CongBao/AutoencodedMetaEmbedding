Question 6(e) NON-ATTRIBUTE: Objects:Atypical Action (noun:verb)

Consider the following word pairs:

recluse:socialize
ascetic:indulge
patron:disparage

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

an X is unlikely to Y
an X once had/owned/possessed Y, but no longer
an X exists in the state of Y
Y are items in a collection/group of X

Correct Answer:

an X is unlikely to Y
