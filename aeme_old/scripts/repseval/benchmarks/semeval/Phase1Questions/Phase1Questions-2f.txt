Question 2(f) PART-WHOLE: Item:Topological Part

Consider the following word pairs:

room:corner
mountain:foot
table:top

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

X causes/compels a person to Y
Y is one of the areas/locations of X
an Y receives an X
an X is a plan for Y

Correct Answer:

Y is one of the areas/locations of X
