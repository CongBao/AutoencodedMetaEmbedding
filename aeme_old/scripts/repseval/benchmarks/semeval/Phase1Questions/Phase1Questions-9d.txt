Question 9(d) SPACE-TIME: Location:Instrument/Associated Item

Consider the following word pairs:

school:textbook
farm:tractor
beach:swimsuit

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

a Y is one item in a collection/group of X
X is the name for knowledge of Y
Y is an instrumental item in the activities that occur at place/location/area Y
an X has the attribute Y

Correct Answer:

Y is an instrumental item in the activities that occur at place/location/area Y
