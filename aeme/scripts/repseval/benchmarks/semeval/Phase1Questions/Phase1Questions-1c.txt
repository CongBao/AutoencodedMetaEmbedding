Question 1(c) CLASS-INCLUSION: Singular Collective

Consider the following word pairs:

cutlery:spoon
clothing:shirt
vermin:rat

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

a Y receives an item/knowledge/service from X
a Y is one item in a collection/group of X
Y is an unacceptable form of X
X is a Y kind of action

Correct Answer:

a Y is one item in a collection/group of X
