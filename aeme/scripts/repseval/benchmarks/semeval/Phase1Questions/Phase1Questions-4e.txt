Question 4(e) CONTRAST: Incompatible

Consider the following word pairs:

happy:morbid
vigilant:careless
slow:stationary

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

Being X is incompatible with being Y
someone will X an object that is Y
Y describes a condition or state that is usually absent from X
Y describes a condition or state that is usually absent from X

Correct Answer:

Being X is incompatible with being Y
