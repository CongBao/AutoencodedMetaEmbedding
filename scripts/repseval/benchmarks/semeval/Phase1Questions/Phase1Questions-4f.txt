Question 4(f) CONTRAST: Asymmetric Contrary

Consider the following word pairs:

hot:cool
destitute:rich
tiny:large
dry:moist

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

Y is a kind/type/instance of X
X is the name for knowledge of Y
X and Y are at opposite ends of the same scale, but X is more extreme than Y
an X makes Y / an X uses Y to make an item

Correct Answer:

X and Y are at opposite ends of the same scale
