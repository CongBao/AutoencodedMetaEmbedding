Question 2(b) PART-WHOLE: Collection:Member

Consider the following word pairs:

forrest:tree
anthology:poem
fleet:ship

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

X is made from a collection of Y
someone perform the action X on Y
an X is unlikely to Y
X and Y are at opposite ends of the same scale, but X is more extreme than Y

Correct Answer:

X is made from a collection of Y
