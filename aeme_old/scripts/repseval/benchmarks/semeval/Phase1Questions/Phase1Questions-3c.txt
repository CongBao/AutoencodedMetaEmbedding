Question 3(c) SIMILAR: Dimensional Excessive

Consider the following word pairs:

eating:gluttony
concerned:obsessed
bleeding:hemorrhage

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

Being X is incompatible with being Y
X is made of / is comprised of Y
someone/something will X in order to Y
Y is an excessive form of X

Correct Answer:

Y is an excessive form of X
