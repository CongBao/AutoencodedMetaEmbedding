Question 7(a) CASE RELATIONS: Agent:Object

Consider the following word pairs:

tailor:suit
oracle:prophesy
baker:flour

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

something that is X cannot be Y
an X makes Y / an X uses Y to make an item
X is intended to produce Y
X enables the use of Y

Correct Answer:

an X makes Y / an X uses Y to make an item
