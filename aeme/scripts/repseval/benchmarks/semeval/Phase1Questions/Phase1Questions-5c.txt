Question 5(c) ATTRIBUTE: ObjectState(noun:noun)

Consider the following word pairs:

beggar:poverty
dupe:gullibility
novice:inexperience

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

an X exists in the state of Y
Y describes a condition or state that is usually absent from X
a Y receives an item/knowledge/service from X
a Y is used on an X

Correct Answer:

an X exists in the state of Y
