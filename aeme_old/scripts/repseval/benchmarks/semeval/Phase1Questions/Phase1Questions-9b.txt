Question 9(b) SPACE-TIME: Location:Process/Product

Consider the following word pairs:

bakery:bread
quarry:rock
laboratory:science

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

an X and Y are two kinds in a category of actions/things/attributes
an X is a place/location/area where Y is made/done/produced
an Y receives an X
an X exists in the state of Y

Correct Answer:

an X is a place/location/area where Y is made/done/produced
