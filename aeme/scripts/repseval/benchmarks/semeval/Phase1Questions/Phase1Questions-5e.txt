Question 5(e) ATTRIBUTE: Object:Typical Action (noun.verb)

Consider the following word pairs:

glass:break
soldier:fight
juggernaut:crush

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

X and Y share a contiguous border
X is a time when Y occurs
an X will typically Y
Y is one of the areas/locations of X

Correct Answer:

an X will typically Y
