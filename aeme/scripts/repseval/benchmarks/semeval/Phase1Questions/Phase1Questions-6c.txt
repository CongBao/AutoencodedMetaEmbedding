Question 6(c) NON-ATTRIBUTE: Object:Nonstate (noun:noun)

Consider the following word pairs:

laureate:dishonor
famine:plenitude
war:tranquility
desert:lushness

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

X is an expression that indicates Y
Y describes a condition or state that is usually absent from X
an X makes Y / an X uses Y to make an item
an X uses Y to perform their role

Correct Answer:

Y describes a condition or state that is usually absent from X
