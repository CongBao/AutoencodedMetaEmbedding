Question 4(a) CONTRAST: Contradictory

Consider the following word pairs:

alive:dead
masculinity:femininity
remember:forget

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

an X uses Y to perform their role
Something cannot be/have/do X and Y at the same time
X is an expression that indicates Y
X and Y are two distinct objects in the same category

Correct Answer:

Something cannot be/have/do X and Y at the same time
