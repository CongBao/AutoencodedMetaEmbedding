Question 2(j) PART-WHOLE: Item:Ex-part/Ex-possession

Consider the following word pairs:

apostate:belief
wood:splinter
prisoner:freedom

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

an X once had/owned/possessed Y, but no longer
a Y receives an item/knowledge/service from X
an X has the attribute Y
an X uses Y to perform their role

Correct Answer:

an X once had/owned/possessed Y
