Question 9(a) SPACE-TIME: Item:Location

Consider the following word pairs:

arsenal:weapon
seminary:theologian
bookshelf:books

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

X and Y are contrary / opposite to each other
an X is a place/location/area where Y is found
a person who is X often is in a state of Y
Y is an instrument through with X receives some object/service/role

Correct Answer:

an X is a place/location/area where Y is found
