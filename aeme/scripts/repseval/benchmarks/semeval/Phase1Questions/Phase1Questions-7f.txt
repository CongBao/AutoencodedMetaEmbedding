Question 7(f) CASE RELATIONS: Object:Recipient

Consider the following word pairs:

inheritance:heir
speech:audience
honor:laureate

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

an X exists in the state of Y
an X indicates/signifies Y
Y is an instrumental item in the activities that occur at place/location/area Y
an Y receives an X

Correct Answer:

an Y receives an X
