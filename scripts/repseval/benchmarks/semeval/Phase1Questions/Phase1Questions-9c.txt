Question 9(c) SPACE-TIME: Location:Action/Activity

Consider the following word pairs:

school:learning
gym:exercise
highway:driving

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

X is the opposite direction from Y
Y describes a condition or state that is usually absent from X
Something cannot be/have/do X and Y at the same time
an X is a place/location/area where Y takes place

Correct Answer:

an X is a place/location/area where Y takes place
