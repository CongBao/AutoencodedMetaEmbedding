Question 8(b) CAUSE-PURPOSE: Cause:Compensatory Action

Consider the following word pairs:

hunger:eat
fatigue:sleep
lateness:hurry

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

X is a Y kind of action
X and Y are two distinct objects in the same category
X causes/compels a person to Y
an X has the attribute Y

Correct Answer:

X causes/compels a person to Y
