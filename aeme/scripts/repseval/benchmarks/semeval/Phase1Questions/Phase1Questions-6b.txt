Consider the following word pairs:

brittle:molded
inconsolable:comforted
exemplary:criticized

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

an X is is a defect in Y
Y is typically found at an event such as X
something that is X cannot be Y
X causes/compels a person to Y
