Question 4(d) CONTRAST: Directional

Consider the following word pairs:

front:back
left:right
east:west
before:after
inside:outside

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

X causes/compels a person to Y
X is one step/action/part of the actions in Y
X possesses/owns/has Y
X is the opposite direction from Y

Correct Answer:

X is the opposite direction from Y
