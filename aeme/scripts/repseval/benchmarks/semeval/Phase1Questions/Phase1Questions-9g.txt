Question 9(g) SPACE-TIME: Time Associated Item

Consider the following word pairs:

retirement:pension
infancy:cradle
adolescence:textbooks

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

something that is X may be Y
Y is an item associated with time X
Y describes a condition or state that is usually absent from X
an X is unlikely to Y

Correct Answer:

Y is an item associated with time X
