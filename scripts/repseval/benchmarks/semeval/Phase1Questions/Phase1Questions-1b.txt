Question 1(b) CLASS-INCLUSION: Functional

Consider the following word pairs:

ornament:brooch
weapon:knife
vehicle:car

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

the result of action X does not produce an object with Y
Y is an instrumental item in the activities that occur at place/location/area Y
Y functions as an X
X possesses/owns/has Y

Correct Answer:

Y functions as an X
