Question 6(f) NON-ATTRIBUTE: Agent/Object Attribute: Atypical Action (adjective:verb)

Consider the following word pairs:

reticent:talk
obtrusive:ignore
garbled:comprehend

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

Y is a kind/type/instance of X
an X causes Y
someone/something who is X is unlikely to Y
someone perform the action X on Y

Correct Answer:

someone/something who is X is unlikely to Y
