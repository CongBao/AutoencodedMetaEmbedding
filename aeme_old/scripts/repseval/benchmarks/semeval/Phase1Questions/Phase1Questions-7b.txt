Question 7(b) CASE RELATIONS: Agent:Recipient

Consider the following word pairs:

doctor:patient
mentor:protege
judge:litigant

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

Y is a kind/type/instance of X
X enables the use of Y
a Y receives an item/knowledge/service from X
Y is a specific X

Correct Answer:

a Y receives an item/knowledge/service from X
