Question 6(g) NON-ATTRIBUTE: Action:Action Nonattribute

Consider the following word pairs:

creep:fast
fade:abruptly
scream:quietly
destroy:gently

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

X enables the use of Y
an X is is a defect in Y
X cannot be done in a Y manner
Y functions as an X

Correct Answer:

X cannot be done in a Y manner
