Question 2(i) PART-WHOLE: Item:Distinctive Nonpart

Consider the following word pairs:

tundra:tree
horse:wings
perfection:fault

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

Y is a kind/type/instance of X
a Y is one item in a collection/group of X
a Y is used on an X
X is devoid of / cannot have Y

Correct Answer:

X is devoid of / cannot have Y
