Question 5(a) ATTRIBUTE: ItemAttribute(noun:adjective)

Consider the following word pairs:

beggar:poor
glass:fragile
hero:brave

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

X cannot be done in a Y manner
an X has the attribute Y
Y is a kind/type/instance of X
X is similar to the opposite of Y, but X is not truly the opposite of Y

Correct Answer:

an X has the attribute Y
