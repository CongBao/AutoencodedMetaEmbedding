Question 10(f) REFERENCE: Concealment

Consider the following word pairs:

alias:name
camouflage:location
disguise:identity

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

X conceals a person/place/thing's Y
Y functions as an X
a Y is used on an X
an X is attached to a Y

Correct Answer:

X conceals a person/place/thing's Y
