Question 4(g) CONTRAST: Pseudoantonym

Consider the following word pairs:

popular:shy. right:bad
believe:deny

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

something/someone that is X will typically Y
X is similar to the opposite of Y, but X is not truly the opposite of Y
Y functions as an X
a Y represents/is representative of X

Correct Answer:

X is similar to the opposite of Y
