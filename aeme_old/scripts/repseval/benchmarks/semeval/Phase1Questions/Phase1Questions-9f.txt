Question 9(f) SPACE-TIME: Time Action/Activity

Consider the following word pairs:

summer:harvest
childhood:play
spring:graduation

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

an X indicates/signifies Y
X is a time when Y occurs
X conceals a person/place/thing's Y
someone/something who is X cannot be Y or be in the state of Y

Correct Answer:

X is a time when Y occurs
