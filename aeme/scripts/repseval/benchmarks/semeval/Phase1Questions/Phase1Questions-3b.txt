Question 3(b) SIMILAR: Dimensional Similarity

Consider the following word pairs:

enthusiasm:fervor
simmer:boil
stream:river

What relation best describes these X:Y word pairs?

an X and Y are two kinds in a category of actions/things/attributes
X is intended to produce Y
an X indicates/signifies Y
an X has the attribute Y

Correct Answer:

an X and Y are two kinds in a category of actions/things/attributes
